Amazing 5 minute interview with Zenia Alarcon

...interview by Vianey Morris

Today, I sat with my friend Zenia in the great dorm of Pine Oak on the West Campus of St. Mark's School. Zenia is from Brooklyn, NY but accordidnf to her, she "doesn't have an accent," (she does). She is good at ceramics and playing softball. She also like rubik's cubes and has several solved cubes in her dorm room.

What is your favorite food? Why?

Zenia likes lasagna because the meat, cheese, and sauce, is "just yummy."

What is your favorite music? Why do you like it?

She enjoys pop music and rap music because it put her in a really good mood.

What is your favorite vacation place? Why? Do you have any specific memories that you can share?

She loved the Disney cruise. She said it was fun having alone time but also quality family time.

What is one thing in the world that you would like to accomplish?

When Zenia gets older, she would like to have a successful job.

What is one thing in the world that you would like to change?

If she could change one thing, it would be racism.

What is one thing in the world that makes you most happy?

The one thing that makes Zenia most happy is dancing.
